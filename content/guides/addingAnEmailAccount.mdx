## Adding an email Account to the Jolla Mind 2

<Callout type="important" emoji="ðŸª²">
    There are still some known issues take a look at them [here](https://github.com/venhoai/jolla-mind2/issues).
</Callout>

<Callout>
    Only SMTP & IMAP are supported at the moment.
</Callout>
<Steps>
    ## Open the `Messaging Center` in the web GUI
    ## Click on `"Add Account+"` in the top right corner
    ## Configure it from the options
    <Callout>
        If you are using 2FA or an Passkey for your Mail Account you will need to use an `App Password` for this.
    </Callout>
    - `Link existing account` ?
    - `New account` Select your email Provider from the Drop-down or set it up manually<br />
    - `Set up manually` Enter your mail server configuration (check for the `IMAP` and `SMTP` fields what your email provider suggests).
    ## Click on `Add` to finish the setup of a new Mail Account
</Steps>
