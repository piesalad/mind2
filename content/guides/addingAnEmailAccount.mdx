<callout>
    Only SMTP & IMAP are supported at the moment.
</callout>

>[!TIP]
>
>If you already have an email account setup, you can add another one via the filter options in the top right:
>**Filter -> add Account**
- Open the `Messaging Center` in the web GUI.
- Click on `"Setup new Inbox"`.
- Enter your mail server configuration (check for the `IMAP` and `SMTP` fields what your email provider suggests).
- Click on `Done` to finish the Setup of a new Mail Account.
