## Messaging Center
>[!Note]
>
>- Currently only new Emails are added.
>- Messaging Center will get more features.
>- Email summery is broken.
>- Processing of new emails is broken if you update any old email (for example: mark as read, delete or archive).

The Messaging Center provides an overview of all the emails, that you recently got.
It will provide a summarization of every email and tag them into a category.
