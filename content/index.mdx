# Welcome to my Documentation for the Jolla Mind 2

# What is the Jolla Mind 2

![Picture of the Jolla Mind2](https://docs-mind2.s3.cubbit.eu/jolla-mind-2.webp)

The [Jolla Mind2](https://www.jollamind2.com/) is a personal AI computer designed to serve as your private digital assistant,
emphasizing data privacy and security.

Are you are looking for the Official Jolla Mind 2 Documentation? <br />
It's on [GitHub](https://github.com/venhoai/jolla-mind2), there you can also create bug reports and suggest/request future features.

If you have any other questions or need help, just join the [Jolla Mind 2 Discord Server](https://discord.gg/CjGEnzWzGm) and ask them there.
